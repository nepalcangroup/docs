## Announcement List

Displays all announcements that have been made.  
Each announcement entry shows the **Title**, **Visible To**, **Added By**, and **Created Date**.

## Add Announcement

You can create a new announcement by clicking the **"New Announcement"** button and filling in the required fields.

## Announcement Details

Shows complete details of the selected announcement, including who it is visible to, who created it, and the content.

## Edit Announcement

Used to update an existing announcement as needed.
