---
title: # Warehouse
---

## Warehouse List

Displays the complete list of warehouses that have been added.

## Add Warehouse

Click the **"New Warehouse"** button located at the top-right corner to create a new warehouse.

## Edit Warehouse

You can edit an existing warehouse to update its details according to your preferences.

## Warehouse Details

View detailed information about each warehouse, including its location, status, and other relevant data.
