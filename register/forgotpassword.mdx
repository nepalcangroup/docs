## "Forgot Password"

If you've forgotten your password, follow these steps to reset it:

## Step 1: Navigate to Forgot Password

- On the login screen, click on **"Forgot Password?"**.
- You will be redirected to the password recovery page.

## Step 2: Enter Your Email Address

- Provide the **email address** associated with your account.
- An OTP (One-Time Password) will be sent to your email.

## Step 3: Enter the OTP

- Check your email inbox for the OTP.
- Enter the received OTP in the designated field on the screen.

## Step 4: Reset Your Password

- After verifying the OTP, you'll be prompted to set a **new password**.
- Enter your new password and confirm it.

Your password has now been successfully updated. You can log in using your new credentials.
