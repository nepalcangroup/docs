# Category

You can add a **product category** from the "Add Category" section.

- After adding the category, it will be available as an option when adding a new product.

![Favicon Image](/images/website/category.png)

- Once successfully added, the category will also appear in the **Product List** under the "Category" column.
  ![Favicon Image](/images/website/categorylist.png)

- You can view category-specific information in the **Product Category Detail** section, where all details related to that category are displayed.
  ![Favicon Image](/images/website/categorydetail.png)
