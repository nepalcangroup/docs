title: "Register for a New Account (Customer Portal)"
---

To access the Customer Portal, you first need to register and create an account. Follow the steps below to complete your registration process.

---

## Registration Form

Fill out the registration form with the following details:

- **Name**
- **Email**
- **Phone Number**
- **Address**
- **Password**

After completing the form, click the **Register** button.
![Favicon Image](/images/customersignin.png)

> If you already have an account, you can click the **Sign In** button below the form to log into your account.

---

## Email Verification

After you click **Register**, you will be navigated to an email verification page.

1. An **OTP (One-Time Password)** will be sent to your registered email address.
2. Enter the OTP into the input field to verify your email address.

## Successful Registration

Once you enter the correct OTP, your account will be successfully created and you will be redirected to your **Customer Profile Dashboard**.
